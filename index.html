Cranial Fronto Nasal Dysplaysia (CFND) is a rare genetic condition which affects the physical appearance of the head and face.

It effects females more than males although males can be carriers with minimal symptoms.

CFND is caused by a mutated EFNB1 gene.
CFND affect 1 in every 100,000 people.
